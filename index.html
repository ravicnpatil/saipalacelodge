About Us

Sai Palace Lodge, Bidar

Sai Palace Lodge is a well-known boarding and lodging establishment located opposite the New Bus Stand in Bidar, Karnataka. Established in 2006, we have been serving guests for nearly 20 years with clean, comfortable, and affordable accommodation for families, business travelers, pilgrims, and tourists visiting the historic city of Bidar.

Just a 5-minute walk from Gurudwara Sri Nanak Jhira Sahib, our central location makes it easy to explore Bidar Fort, Mahmud Gawan Madrasa, and other famous attractions. We provide a range of rooms to suit every budget — from budget-friendly non-AC rooms starting at ₹500 to premium AC rooms.

Bidar Fort

A magnificent 15th-century fort with stunning architecture and panoramic views.

Mahmud Gawan Madrasa

Historic Islamic seminary and a masterpiece of Bahmani-era architecture.

Sacred Sikh shrine with a natural spring, attracting devotees from across India.

Bahmani Tombs

Royal tombs of Bahmani sultans with intricate carvings and paintings.
